## 6 Questions to Ask Before Writing a Uniswap V4 Hook (OpenZeppelin)

[6 Questions to Ask Before Writing a Uniswap V4 Hook](https://www.openzeppelin.com/news/6-questions-to-ask-before-writing-a-uniswap-v4-hook) from OpenZeppelin provides a checklist for developers building custom hook contracts for Uniswap v4 pools. It walks through key design considerations—such as pool-hook relationships, whether the hook initiates calls back to the PoolManager, handling liquidity modifications, swap callback symmetry, support for native tokens, and access control—to help ensure secure and correct operation of hooks.
